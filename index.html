About

Welcome to the part where I tell you a little bit about myself! I am currently a senior attending Southwest Career and Technical Academy for Web Design and Development. I have experience with HTML5 and CSS, while also dabbling with JavaScript and PHP from time to time. I am an Adobe Certified Associate in Illustrator CS6, Photoshop CS6, and Flash CS6.

I am currently the Parlimentarian for the 2016-17 SWCTA SkillsUSA chapter. I have competed in a few competitions, earning Bronze at the 2016 SkillsUSA Nevada State Competition for Web Design.

I have a developing passion for art as I have spent the past few years exploring my skills and style. Other than coding and designing, I love to read and write when I find the time. Here, I have chosen to create a collection of my works that have accumulated over the years.
