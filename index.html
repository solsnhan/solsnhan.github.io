About

Welcome to the part where I tell you a little bit about myself! I attended Southwest Career and Technical Academy for Web Design and Development and graduated in 2017. I attended the University of British Columbia for Commerce from 2017 to 2018 and will be attending the University of Nevada, Las Vegas during the fall of 2018. I have experience with HTML5 and CSS, while also dabbling with JavaScript and PHP from time to time. I am an Adobe Certified Associate in Illustrator CS6, Photoshop CS6, and Flash CS6.

I was the Parlimentarian for the SWCTA SkillsUSA chapter from 2015-2017. I have competed in a few competitions, earning Bronze at the 2016 SkillsUSA Nevada State Competition for Web Design and Gold in 2017. I went on to compete in the SkillsUSA National Leadership Conference in 2017 and earned 6th place.

I have a developing passion for art as I have spent the past few years exploring my skills and style. Other than coding and designing, I love to read and write when I find the time. Here, I have chosen to create a collection of my works that have accumulated over the years.
