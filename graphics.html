Self Portrait - Winter 2013

This was an assignment for my Graphic Design I class in 2013; it was used as an introduction to Adobe Illustrator. The goal was to use the pen tool to create a realistic self portrait while having a picture reference.

This was the final assignment to prove my knowledge of the program. I used the pen tool for most of the project, incorporating the gradient and shape tools to add to the overall design. As one might notice, this was created in Adobe Illustrator.

SkillsUSA Pin Design Entry - Spring 2015

This was my entry for the SkillsUSA State Competition Pin Design category during the spring of 2015. The goal was to create a pin design that would represent the state of Nevada.

My inspiration for this design came from the numerous mountains in Nevada and the casino-based industry in Las Vegas. The mountains and sun would represent the natural aspects of the state and the casino chip border would help tie in an important aspect of Nevada. It gave me a new perspective on design and what it would be like designing for different mediums. I used Adobe Illustrator to create this design.

SkillsUSA Web Design Competition Entry (Design) - Spring 2015

This was my entry for the SkillsUSA State Competition Web Design category during the spring of 2015. The competition had four main challenges: HTML, CSS, Javascript, and design. The competition was done in partners and the work was divided and shared. I was in charge of the design challenge that asked for a mockup of a website for a Mexican-German fusion restaurant.

My inspiration for the mockup was the colors of the Mexican and German flag. I encorporated the two countries' colors and tried to create a visually appealing homepage. The fonts were chosen to be easily read and the slide show would provide interaction between the user and the page. I used Adobe Illustrator to create this mockup. It was interesting to work under a small time constraint and see where the design would go.

Brian Head Arts Festival Entry - Spring 2015

This was my entry for the Brian Head Arts Festival Poster Contest during the summer of 2015. The goal was to create a poster that would be able represent Brian Head, Utah and be appropriate for an arts festival. My entry placed 9th overall.

My inspiration started with deers. Brian Head, Utah is known for their nature and national parks. Deers are a commodity in the area. Stars are prominent in the night sky when it comes to nature-orientated areas. I chose to create a deer constellation to represent the natural environment that is well known in Brian Head. I created this piece in Adobe Illustrator.
